The Best Anime According to CherieBrew

My Top 3 Anime Series of All Time

One Piece

An amazingly funny action packed Shounen that slightly touches on real world themes.

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

An isekai story about a guy who got reborn as an overpowered slime.

Inuyasha

A drama filled historical romance between a teenage girl who travels through time to meet a demon boy with dog-ears.
